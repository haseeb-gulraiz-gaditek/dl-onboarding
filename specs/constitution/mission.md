# Mission & Vision

> Part of the Mesh Product Constitution

**Last amended:** 2026-04-27

---

## Mission

Mesh is a personal AI concierge for laypeople navigating AI-tool overload, and a precision launch surface for founders who want to reach the actual users in their niche — not just other founders scrolling launch platforms.

## Vision

Become the canonical user-owned context graph for AI-tooling decisions: a place where any credible launch finds its first hundred real users at workflow-level precision, and where every layperson has a trusted, contextual source for what to adopt and what to skip.

## Context

AI tooling has fragmented into 8,000+ products with no signal-to-noise filter, and existing launch platforms (Product Hunt, HN, "build in public" on X) deliver audiences composed primarily of other founders rather than real end users — receipts: 0.25% PH conversion, ~12 signups per 150K X impressions, Lenny Rachitsky's analysis of 100+ consumer apps shows PH absent from every first-user channel. The two problems compound and are actually the same problem viewed from opposite sides: users can't find relevant tools, founders can't find relevant users. Mesh sits in the middle.
